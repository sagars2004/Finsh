<h3 style="text-align: center; margin-bottom: 30px; color: #2d3436;">Frequently Asked Questions</h3>

<div style="margin-bottom: 60px;">
  <details style="margin-bottom: 15px; background: white; border: 1px solid #e9ecef; border-radius: 8px; overflow: hidden;">
    <summary style="font-weight: 600; cursor: pointer; padding: 20px; color: #2d3436; list-style: none; display: flex; align-items: center; justify-content: space-between;">
      Is my financial data safe?
      <span style="color: #b2bec3;">+</span>
    </summary>
    <div style="padding: 0 20px 20px; color: #636e72; line-height: 1.6;">
      Yes. Your data is stored locally on your device's secure storage. We do not transmit, sync, or back up your personal financial inputs to any external servers. You are the only one who has access to your data.
    </div>
  </details>

  <details style="margin-bottom: 15px; background: white; border: 1px solid #e9ecef; border-radius: 8px; overflow: hidden;">
    <summary style="font-weight: 600; cursor: pointer; padding: 20px; color: #2d3436; list-style: none; display: flex; align-items: center; justify-content: space-between;">
      Why do I need to enter my state?
      <span style="color: #b2bec3;">+</span>
    </summary>
    <div style="padding: 0 20px 20px; color: #636e72; line-height: 1.6;">
      Finsh uses your state of residence to automatically estimate your state and federal tax obligations. This allows us to give you a "real feel" number for your take-home pay, rather than just gross salary.
    </div>
  </details>

  <details style="margin-bottom: 15px; background: white; border: 1px solid #e9ecef; border-radius: 8px; overflow: hidden;">
    <summary style="font-weight: 600; cursor: pointer; padding: 20px; color: #2d3436; list-style: none; display: flex; align-items: center; justify-content: space-between;">
      How do I delete my data?
      <span style="color: #b2bec3;">+</span>
    </summary>
    <div style="padding: 0 20px 20px; color: #636e72; line-height: 1.6;">
      Since all your data is stored locally on your device, restarting the app, or even returning to the homepage, will automatically erase all your data.
    </div>
  </details>

  <details style="margin-bottom: 15px; background: white; border: 1px solid #e9ecef; border-radius: 8px; overflow: hidden;">
    <summary style="font-weight: 600; cursor: pointer; padding: 20px; color: #2d3436; list-style: none; display: flex; align-items: center; justify-content: space-between;">
      Does Finsh work offline?
      <span style="color: #b2bec3;">+</span>
    </summary>
    <div style="padding: 0 20px 20px; color: #636e72; line-height: 1.6;">
      Absolutely. Since Finsh is a "local-first" app, it is fully functional without an internet connection. Think of it as the same caliber as a calculator or weather app that you can just pull up on your phone anytime, anywhere.
    </div>
  </details>

  <details style="margin-bottom: 15px; background: white; border: 1px solid #e9ecef; border-radius: 8px; overflow: hidden;">
    <summary style="font-weight: 600; cursor: pointer; padding: 20px; color: #2d3436; list-style: none; display: flex; align-items: center; justify-content: space-between;">
      Is Finsh free to use?
      <span style="color: #b2bec3;">+</span>
    </summary>
    <div style="padding: 0 20px 20px; color: #636e72; line-height: 1.6;">
      Yes, Finsh is currently completely free to use. Our goal is to provide accessible financial clarity without barriers.
    </div>
  </details>

  <details style="margin-bottom: 15px; background: white; border: 1px solid #e9ecef; border-radius: 8px; overflow: hidden;">
    <summary style="font-weight: 600; cursor: pointer; padding: 20px; color: #2d3436; list-style: none; display: flex; align-items: center; justify-content: space-between;">
      How accurate are the tax estimates?
      <span style="color: #b2bec3;">+</span>
    </summary>
    <div style="padding: 0 20px 20px; color: #636e72; line-height: 1.6;">
      We calculate estimates based on standard federal and state tax brackets to give you a realistic baseline. However, these figures are for planning purposes only and should not replace professional tax advice.
    </div>
  </details>
</div>
